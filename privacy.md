# Moments Ahead Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Moments Ahead is a simple personal countdown app for keeping track of upcoming dates, milestones, goals, trips, anniversaries, and other moments.

## Information we collect

Moments Ahead does not require you to create an account.

The app is designed for personal countdowns and does not ask you to provide personal information to use the core app experience.

If you contact us for support by email, we may receive the information you choose to include in your message, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, or a description of your issue.

## How we use information

Information you send to support is used only to respond to your request, troubleshoot issues, improve the app, and communicate with you about your inquiry.

## Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share support messages except as needed to operate support, comply with law, protect our rights, or resolve technical issues.

## Data storage

Countdowns and app content are intended to remain part of your personal app experience. Support emails are stored by the email service used to receive and respond to support requests.

## Children's privacy

Moments Ahead is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through support, contact us so we can delete it.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
