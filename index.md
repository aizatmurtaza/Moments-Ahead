# Moments Ahead Support

Moments Ahead helps you keep upcoming milestones, trips, goals, anniversaries, and special dates in view with simple countdowns.

## Get help

For support, feedback, or questions about Moments Ahead, email:

momentsaheadsupport@gmail.com

## Common questions

### Do I need an account?

No. Moments Ahead does not require sign-in.

### What can I track?

You can create countdowns for personal events, goals, birthdays, travel, launches, anniversaries, and other dates you want to remember.

### How do I report a problem?

Email support with a short description of what happened, the device you are using, and any steps that help reproduce the issue.

## Privacy

Moments Ahead is designed as a simple personal countdown app. If you need privacy-related support, contact us at the email above.
